FAQS

Storage Category

1. What kind of a container should I store spice in?
The ideal way to store whole & ground spices for longer shelf life is to keep them away from heat, moisture & direct sunlight

2.What is the best way to store spices?
It is best to store your spices in dry, air-tight containers. Glass jars with fitting lids are ideal for cabinets and drawers.

3. Can I store spices in a refrigerator or freezer?
Normally spices and herbs are stored in the refrigerator or fridge to prevent loss of color. However, moisture trapping problem occurs while using the
spices in an ambient area.

4. How long do spices and herbs last?
Whole spices have more stability than ground spices. Normally, whole spices last for 1.5 -2 years and ground spices for 1 -1.5 years, if they’re properly stored in
dry, air tight containers.
